CHICKEN-FILA
Brothers Dan and Bubba Cathy own and run fast-food
chain Chick-fil-A,founded by their father S. Truett Cathy
(d. 2014) in 1967 in Atlanta.Dan is chairman and CEO of
the chain, which has 2,500 outlets--nearly all franchises--
in the U.S.; younger brother Bubba is executive vice
president. Chick-fil-A is known for its chicken sandwiches
and for being closed on Sundays so employees can attend
church; Truett Cathy was a devout Baptist.
